# Selam Wellness Judge Demo Script

## 20-Second Opening

"Selam means peace. Selam Wellness is a digital Ethiopian wellness village for young Ethiopians who need belonging, emotional support, culturally rooted guidance, and real-world healing experiences."

## Problem

Most wellness products feel imported, individualistic, and culturally generic. Ethiopian users already understand healing through community: Idir, Mahber, coffee ceremony, elders, women’s circles, and real-world gathering. Selam brings that into a premium digital product.

## Product Walkthrough

1. **Home / Women's Haven:** show the warm Tibeb design, cycle card, whispers, practitioner CTA, and wellness cards.
2. **Women's Haven:** show the cycle dashboard, protected anonymous post composer, and practitioner match.
3. **Circles:** show category-based discovery and explain calm communities with supportive reactions only.
4. **Retreats:** show the booking cards and explain marketplace revenue from retreats, spas, resorts, and guided rituals.
5. **Backend:** mention NestJS, PostgreSQL schema, moderation safety check, booking API, journal API, practitioner directory, and no-Docker deployment.

## Investor Angle

- **Subscription:** premium Women's Haven, cycle insights, journaling patterns, and advanced wellness library.
- **Marketplace:** commission on retreats, practitioners, spas, and wellness experiences.
- **B2B:** university, employer, and bootcamp mental wellness circles.
- **Data moat:** culturally specific wellness journeys, without selling private journal data.

## Closing

"Selam is not another meditation app. It is a digital Ethiopian wellness village: community, care, wisdom, and commerce in one trusted ecosystem."
